Who we are

From FLL champions to FTC rookies. Building robots. Mentoring teams. Spreading the spark.

Welcome to the home of Team 60317, the Superconducktors. We're an ambitious group of student innovators embarking on our rookie season in the FIRST Tech Challenge — but our team has a rich history of collaboration and competition that began in 5th grade.

Our journey started in FIRST Lego League, where we competed for three consecutive seasons: Masterpiece, Submerged, and Unearthed. Over those three years we evolved from curious beginners into a high-performing unit. Our dedication to creative problem-solving was recognized with the Breakthrough Award, celebrating our team's unique approach to the Innovation Project and our custom robot architecture.

Now we're transitioning from Lego to metal and high-powered electronics — and we're bringing the Superconducktor spark to the FTC field this year.
